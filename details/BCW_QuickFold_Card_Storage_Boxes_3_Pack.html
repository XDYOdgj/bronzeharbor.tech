BCW QuickFold Card Storage Boxes

Dedicated storage for your most prized collectible cards. Whether you're a casual collector or a serious enthusiast, these boxes provide the security, organization, and convenience you need to keep your cards in top condition.

Spacious and Secure

Each QuickFold box is engineered to hold up to 850 standard sports cards or 1200 loose trading cards, making it ideal for managing large collections. The design accommodates both thick and thin cards, offering flexible storage options with a tight fit for maximum protection.

Easy to Use

With a secure magnetic closure, these boxes open and close effortlessly without need for tools. The locking mechanism ensures your cards are safely sealed against dust and damage. Plus, the quick assembly feature allows for simple setup using included adhesive strips.

Convenient for All Needs

These foldable card storage boxes are perfect for collectors who want to keep their cards organized, sorted, or ready for display. The set includes three boxes, giving you the versatility to manage different types of cards or even transport them securely when needed.

Why Choose BCW QuickFold?

Whether you're preparing for a card show, arranging your personal collection, or just looking for a reliable storage solution, the BCW QuickFold Card Storage Boxes are designed to meet your needs. Their collapsible structure makes them easy to store, saving space in your home or office while still offering durable, customizable compartments for all your trading cards.

Perfect for Collectors

These magnetic closure boxes are built for collectors who value ease of access, security, and organization. The compact yet expandable design works seamlessly with most card storage accessories, making it a practical choice for anyone who wants to keep their trading cards protected and neatly arranged.
