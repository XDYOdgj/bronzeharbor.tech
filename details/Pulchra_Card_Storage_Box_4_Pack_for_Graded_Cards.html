Elevate Your Collection with Pulchra Card Storage Boxes

With a larger capacity than most standard options, these boxes are perfect for holding over 90 graded cards, 450 Top Loaders, or 100 OneTouches. The dust-proof surface ensures your cards remain in excellent condition, safeguarding their value for years to come.
