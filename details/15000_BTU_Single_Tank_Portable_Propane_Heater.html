15,000 BTU Portable Propane Heater

Experience reliable outdoor heating with a powerful and easy-to-use propane heater that runs on a single tank. Whether you’re hosting in your backyard, relaxing on your patio, or staying warm during colder nights, this heater gives you the comfort and efficiency you need without the hassle of wires or electricity.

Powerful and Efficient Heating

This heater delivers a robust 15,000 BTU output, making it perfect for quick warmth in large outdoor spaces. Its adjustable heat settings—equipped with 15,000, 12,500, and 10,000 BTU options—ensure you can customize the heat to suit your environment and comfort level.

Portable and Safe Design

Featuring a compact and lightweight build, this heater is easy to transport and perfect for use in garages, sheds, or even on your lawn. It includes a built-in tip-over safety shut-off switch, providing an extra layer of protection when moving or in elevated spaces. Plus, its easy match lit pilot system eliminates the need for electricity, making it a versatile solution for any outdoor setting.

Long Run Time and Convenience

Equipped with approximately 29 hours of run time on the highest setting, you’ll enjoy extended use without needing to refill frequently. Whether you're hosting a gathering, working outdoors, or simply relaxing in the cold, this heater ensures you stay warm, safe, and comfortable.

Perfect for Outdoor Enthusiasts

Designed with simplicity and performance in mind, this heater is a must-have for anyone who needs dependable heat on the go. It’s ideal for patios, lawns, and gardens, providing instant warmth and convenience for your outdoor lifestyle without compromising on safety or efficiency.

Key Features
  • Direct mount to 20 lb propane tank for easy setup
  • Adjustable heat settings: 15,000, 12,500, and 10,000 BTUs
  • Built-in tip-over safety shut-off for added security
